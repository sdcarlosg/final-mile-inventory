User Manual

Manage Employees

1
Accessing the Module: Click on the users icon () in the top bar to open the employee list.
2
Adding Staff: Tap the blue "+" button. You can Scan a Badge ID or enter it manually (8 digits).
3
Identification: Enter the Name and Phone Number. These details are used to link assets to responsible staff.
4
Editing/Deleting: Use the pencil icon to correct data. The trash icon removes an employee, but only if they have no items assigned.
Quick Tip: Use the search bar to find employees instantly by name or badge number.

Manage Inventory

1
Asset Management: Open the module with the clipboard icon (). Here you can see all registered equipment.
2
Registering Assets: Use the blue "+" button. You must provide a unique Asset ID (scan QR or enter manually), Name, and Description.
3
Tracking Status: Assets can be AVAILABLE or IN USE. The system automatically tracks who has what and since when.
4
Actions: Search for any item by Name or ID. Use the edit feature to update specifications or the trash icon to remove assets from the database.
Quick Tip: Keep your Asset IDs clear (e.g., FINL001) for faster scanning during assignments.

Asset Assignment

1
Select Employee: Search by name or scan their Badge ID. This identifies who is receiving the equipment.
2
Select Assets: You can scan multiple items one after another or select them from the list of AVAILABLE items.
3
Batch Scanned Items: You'll see a list of items ready to be assigned at the bottom. You can remove any item if scanned by mistake.
4
Finish: Tap "Assign Items". The system updates their status to IN USE and records the assignment date.
Quick Tip: Use a physical scanner or the camera for Step 2 to assign multiple items in seconds.

Return Assets

1
Identify User: Select the employee who is returning the items. The system will display all items currently assigned to them.
2
Select Items: Scan the asset QR or select from their list of active assignments. You can return multiple items at once.
3
Confirm Check-in: Tap "Return Items". The assets will automatically return to AVAILABLE status.
Quick Tip: Use the camera scanner to quickly verify the physical ID of the item being returned.

Report Issue

1
Find Asset: Search for the item being reported. Only items currently IN USE can be reported as damaged or missing.
2
Describe Incident: Enter a brief note explaining what happened (e.g., "Screen cracked" or "Lost during transit").
3
Update Status: Submit the report. The item's condition will be updated to DAMAGED or recorded as an incident.
Important: Damaged items remain assigned to the user until they are physically returned through the "Return Item" module.

Cloud Sync

Updates are saved locally and synced automatically with Google Sheets when you see the green cloud icon ().
